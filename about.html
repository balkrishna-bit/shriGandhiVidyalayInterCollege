About Shri Gandhi Vidyalay Inter College, Gonda

About Us

The school was established on 19 February 1946 by Late Shri Nand Lal Verma in memory of Mahatma Gandhi with the aim of providing quality and well-organized education to the children of railway employees as well as other members of the community. The institution received recognition as a Junior School in 1960, High School recognition in 1962, Intermediate (Science Stream) recognition in 1969, and Intermediate (Humanities Stream) recognition in 1974. The remarkable growth and development of the school was largely guided by Late Shri J. P. Srivastava, who served the institution from July 1960 to September 1987 and played a pivotal role in its progress. Today, the school is committed to providing high-quality education from the Primary to the Intermediate level, offering comprehensive academic programs in the Humanities, Science, and Vocational streams while nurturing the intellectual, moral, and overall development of its students.

About Our Principal

Major Dr. Rajesh Dwivedi

Principal
Shri Gandhi Vidyalay Inter College, Gonda

Education is not merely about acquiring knowledge; it is about developing character, values, discipline, and the confidence to face life's challenges. At our institution, we strive to create a learning environment where every student is encouraged to explore their potential, think independently, and grow into responsible citizens. Since our establishment in 1947, the school has remained committed to providing quality education while preserving the ideals of integrity, respect, and excellence. Our dedicated teachers work tirelessly to ensure that every student receives the guidance and opportunities needed for academic as well as personal growth. I firmly believe that every child possesses unique talents, and it is our responsibility to nurture those talents through proper guidance, modern teaching practices, and continuous encouragement. Together with the support of parents and the community, we aim to prepare our students for a bright and successful future. — Major Dr. Rajesh Dwivedi
